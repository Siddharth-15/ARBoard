Delving into the future of augmented collaboration and the minds behind it.

The Next Dimension of Ideation

AR Whiteboard transcends traditional boundaries, merging the tactile familiarity of a whiteboard with the limitless potential of augmented reality. It's not just about viewing information; it's about interacting with it in a shared, spatial context.

Our platform is engineered for seamless real-time collaboration, allowing multiple users to co-create, annotate, and visualize ideas simultaneously. Whether you're in the same room or across the globe, AR Whiteboard provides an intuitive and immersive environment to bring concepts to life, project them into your world, and foster unparalleled understanding.

Discover Key Features

Core Capabilities

Real-Time Sync

Experience instant updates as multiple users draw, write, and modify the canvas simultaneously. Powered by robust WebSocket technology.

Immersive AR Mode

Project your digital whiteboard onto any flat surface in your real-world environment for unparalleled spatial understanding and presentation.

Persistent Sessions

Save your work, including all pages and edits. Resume your sessions anytime, right where you left off, ensuring no idea is lost.

Multi-Page Canvases

Organize complex ideas across multiple pages within a single session, easily navigating between different stages of your project.

Rich Toolset

Utilize a comprehensive suite of drawing tools, shapes, text options, color palettes, undo/redo, and more for expressive ideation.

Secure & Modern

Built with modern web technologies, ensuring a secure, responsive, and intuitive experience across devices with dark/light mode support.
